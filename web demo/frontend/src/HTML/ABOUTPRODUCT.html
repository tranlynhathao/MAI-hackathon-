1. Appointment Booking for Medical Check-ups and Vaccinations: The system allows users to schedule appointments for medical check-ups and vaccinations online through a mobile application or website. Users can choose their preferred doctor, date, time, and location from a list of participating healthcare facilities.

2. Create an Account and Store Information: Each patient will have a personal account within the system where they can store all health-related information. The system will automatically update the patient's profile with each medical visit, including medical history, test results, prescribed medications, and details of services used.

KEY FEATURES:

1. Enhanced Personal Health Management: By storing health information online, users can easily track their health status, enabling them to self-manage and make informed decisions about their health.

2. Time-saving and Convenience: Booking appointments and managing health information online reduces wait times at hospitals and increases convenience for users.

3. Improved Quality of Healthcare Services: The system helps improve information management and enhances the efficiency of healthcare facilities by providing convenient access to patient health data, ensuring consistency and security of information.

4. This project not only contributes to achieving Goal 3 of the United Nations but also positively impacts the quality of life and health of the community.
